Dynamic Charging Roads for Electric Vehicles

This project aims to develop an innovative solution for dynamically charging electric vehicles while they are in motion. The concept involves embedding wireless charging technology into roadways, allowing electric vehicles to recharge their batteries without needing to stop. The system utilizes electromagnetic fields to transfer energy to the vehicle's battery, potentially reducing range anxiety and supporting the widespread adoption of electric vehicles.

Current Status: Under Development
